# Metrics

## Main metric

Immediate LGTM rate: submissions approved on first review and merged without revision requests.

## Before

- About 1 of 5 submissions passed without extensive review iteration.
- Approximate immediate approval rate: 20%.

## After

- 5 of 7 consecutive submissions received immediate LGTM.
- Approximate immediate approval rate: 71%.

## Interpretation

This is a process-quality signal, not a controlled experiment. Possible factors include improved author experience, clearer CI expectations, reusable patterns, and systematic failure prevention.

The rate increased by about 3.5x. The practical effect was fewer review cycles.
