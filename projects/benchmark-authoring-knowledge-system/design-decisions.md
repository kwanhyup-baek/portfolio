# Design Decisions

## Publish the system, not task files

The public case study describes the method without exposing proprietary artifacts.

## Reuse reviewer feedback

Recurring comments become design rules rather than one-time corrections.

## Compress CI behavior into notes

Compact notes make validation constraints easier to apply during authoring.

## Keep examples separate from rules

Examples show patterns. Rules state reusable constraints. This separation makes updates clearer.

## Optimize for immediate LGTM

Eventual approval can include several revision cycles. First-review approval better reflects review-ready work.
