# AI Systems & Evaluation Engineering Portfolio

A collection of AI systems, evaluation workflows, and automation platforms independently designed and implemented to solve real-world bottlenecks in evaluation, experimentation, knowledge management, and agentic workflows.

Rather than relying solely on dedicated commercial platforms, these projects focus on designing lightweight, task-specific systems using custom workflows, automation pipelines, local knowledge stores, and reproducible engineering practices.

The portfolio emphasizes:

- System design
- Evaluation engineering
- Workflow automation
- Knowledge management
- Experiment management
- Agentic systems

Proprietary code, prompts, datasets, and internal artifacts are excluded.

## Projects

### [Benchmark Authoring Knowledge System](projects/benchmark-authoring-knowledge-system/)

An independently designed knowledge-management system for multilingual benchmark authoring.

The project transformed CI requirements, reviewer feedback, and successful task patterns into reusable design guidance.

Over the course of the project, first-review approval efficiency increased from approximately 20% to 71%, with 5 of 7 consecutive submissions receiving immediate LGTM approval and merging without additional review iterations.

### [Lightweight LLMOps Experiment Platform](projects/lightweight-llmops-experiment-platform/)

A lightweight experiment-management and optimization platform for LLM systems.

Combines daytime human-in-the-loop refinement with nighttime autonomous experimentation, featuring artifact tracking, parameter optimization, knowledge transfer between cycles, and self-improving optimization workflows inspired by modern LLMOps platforms.
